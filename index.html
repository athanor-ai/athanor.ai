What we work on

Core research areas

Deterministic training environments for AI agents, grounded in formal methods, programming languages, and software verification.

01
Verifiable Rewards
Every score comes from a compiler, type checker, model checker, or test suite. No LLM judges, no rubrics. The verifier is the reward.
02
Formal Methods at Scale
Theorem proving, hardware verification, protocol correctness, systems migration. Tasks that require multi-step reasoning with formal guarantees.
03
Calibrated Difficulty
Every environment is tuned so no frontier model solves everything. Configurable scoring gives dense training signal for RL and strict evaluation for benchmarking.
Interactive demo

Scoring calibrator

Every environment ships with configurable scoring. Move the slider to shift between generous partial credit for RL training and strict pass/fail for evaluation. Scores are recomputed live from real baseline runs across 5 frontier models and 6 environments.
